Exodus Mission

The Exodus mission is an interplanetary journey composed of 80 pioneers and a captain : myself, sent to populate our new planet Mars. We are the only hope to save humanity from its imminent destruction. Our ultimate ambition is to colonize Mars in order to save Humanity, which needs a new home to support its species. It is the first mission of this type so obviously it implies some risks like the shuttle crashing, damage due to impacts in space, or even lack of oxygen or vital supplies. It is also a harsh psychological experience as eighty persons will find themselves in a confined space for a very long time.
